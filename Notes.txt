Paymet Gateway
Guidelines
- Credit card and online net banking
- Secure

Limitations/Challenges
- accepts all cards
- International payments
- Security threat

Gateway Type-
- Redirect to Gateway (paypal)
- 3rd party gateway (onsite cart checkout, offsite payment)
- In-house Gateway (on-site payment)

Example-
- Paypal
- Stripe
- Braintree

- sandbox development/testing env.
- Generate client credentials/Token
- Execute payment
